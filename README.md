The **LinkedIn Company URL - Mass Profile Finder** automates the process of finding official LinkedIn company pages for a list of businesses. Instead of manually searching for each company, it quickly compiles LinkedIn URLs, saving time and effort.

### Why This Matters

- Lets recruiters and job seekers instantly access company pages for outreach or applications.
- Helps marketers and sales teams create verified lists of companies to target.

## What Data This Scraper Extracts

| Field Name | Field Description |
|-------------|------------------|
| companyName | The name of the company as provided in input. |
| linkedinUrl | The LinkedIn profile URL of the company. |
| info | Additional metadata or result information for the matched company. |
| searchQuery | The search term used to find the company page. |
| timestamp | Date and time when the data was scraped. |

## FAQs

**Q1: Do I need a LinkedIn account to use this scraper?**
No, it operates without requiring authentication or cookies, making it safer and simpler to run at scale.

**Q2: How many companies can I process at once?**
You can scrape thousands of companies in one batch, with free usage allowing up to 20,000 results monthly.

**Q3: What output formats are supported?**
You can export results in JSON, CSV, Excel, XML, or RSS formats for maximum flexibility.

**Q4: Is scraping LinkedIn data legal?**
Collecting publicly available LinkedIn company URLs is generally allowed, but avoid scraping personal or private data. Always comply with data protection laws such as GDPR.
